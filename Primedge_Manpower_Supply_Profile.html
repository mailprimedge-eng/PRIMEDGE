Mrs. Bindi Dubey PROPRIETOR & FOUNDER  |  100% OWNERSHIP

Primedge Manpower & Supply is a proprietorship enterprise founded on 13 March 2026 by Mrs. Bindi Dubey, and is registered as a Micro Enterprise under the Udyam scheme of the Ministry of MSME, Government of India.

The firm was established to deliver dependable manpower and institutional support services to corporate, commercial and government establishments — with full ownership, control and decision-making resting solely with its woman proprietor.

Every engagement is personally overseen by the founder, ensuring accountability and consistent service quality from the very first deployment.
